New checks were introduced by SAP Note 2006072 (or corresponding Support package of EA-PS). Because of this, it is necessary to analyze the FM update profile details in combination with your settings for Closing Operations (CCF) in Funds Management.

\n

For this procedure, you can start report RFFMMONI from transaction code SE38 to verify which kind of profile are you using:

\n
    \n
  • Period-Based Encumbrance Tracking (PBET).  
    • \n
  • Annual/Fiscal Year-Based Encumbrance Tracking (ABET/YBET).
    • \n
\n

In addition to that, you will need to verify your settings for Commitment Carryforward (CCF) in the IMG activities for Closing operations, to determine if Carryforward (CCF) of Value type 54 is allowed:
Public Sector Management -> Funds Management Government -> Closing Operations
Enter Default Settings
and
Commitment Closing Operations -> Enter Settings for Commitment Carryforward Payment Budget

\n

By knowing those settings, combined with your FM update profile information, you can identify one of the 4 possible solutions.
